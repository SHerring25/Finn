Kenshin Himura

One of my favorite anime shows is Rurouni Kenshin. He is a very skilled swordsman, who posses a unique sword. His sword is a reverse blade sword; meaning it is not meant to be used to kill, but if it comes down to a life and death battle then Kenshin simply reverses the blade around for a kill strike. I really enjoy the action and suspense in the show and also the comedy they have in some parts. This is one of the shows I would request someone to watch, if they want to get into anime.
